Users of the service may be billed approximately two months after the services. Thus, PIs must plan accordingly and set aside funds such that they are available at the time of billing.

Prices are per lane and include cluster generation, sequencing run, base calls, and quality control reports. To minimize costs for researchers, pricing per lane is based upon full occupancy of the flow cell (8 lanes) per sequencing run. As indicated on the introduction page, researchers are responsible for the costs and the preparation of libraries for sequencing.
